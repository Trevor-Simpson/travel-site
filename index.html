Welcome!

Hawaii has many tourist attractions to bring in visitors. For example the Volcanoes National Park, the island of Waikiki, Mauna Kia, Hana Road, Maui Ocean Park and the beautiful Pacific Ocean.

Whether you're planning your honeymoon or a simple vacation, you'll need to plan ahead if you want to spend your time in Hawaii because staying there can get pricey. According to AAA, the average cost per day for a trip for two to Hawaii runs about $793 for just food and lodging, as of 2008.

I want to visit Hawaii not just because it's a beautiful tropical island but because I want to visit the top of Mt. Mauna Kea where you can see a beautiful view of the Milky Way Galaxy.
